  Aesthetic: Editorial-scientific zine. Think Nature journal meets a 90s
  European design monograph with halftone-collage punctuation. The brain
  stickers are not decoration — they're the fixture, collaged into the hero like
   figure plates.

  Color (OKLCH, Committed strategy):
  - paper — oklch(0.94 0.006 85) — warm newsprint off-white
  - ink — oklch(0.18 0.010 60) — warm near-black
  - ember — oklch(0.68 0.22 45) — flame orange from the stickers (accent, ~8%)
  - cortex — oklch(0.56 0.020 280) — cool graphite for secondary
  - pulse — oklch(0.62 0.19 22) — blood-red, reserved for conflict/friction
  - signal — oklch(0.82 0.14 95) — caution yellow, reserved for flagged
  anomalies

  Type:
  - Display: Fraunces (variable, optical-size) — editorial serif, distinctive
  - Body: IBM Plex Sans — industrial, not generic
  - Meta: IBM Plex Mono — data labels, stage numbers, z-scores

  Structural moves:
  - Running issue header with date/issue-no + halftone row
  - Oversized display headline overlapping the brain sticker
  - Figure-plate captions (FIG. 01 — cortical response)
  - Polygraph-style emotion timelines that draw in as stage completes
  - No cards. Horizontal rules, numbered indices, marginalia instead.
